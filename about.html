About Chirpsearch

Chirpsearch is a reimaginging of the Twitter Advanced Search page, with a focus on making it fast and user friendly, as well as adding mobile support that was lacking in the original. Searches are constructed based on parameters and input from users before they are sent to Twitter's existing search results page. We use cookies and local storage to maintain a record of your customizations and past options used, which are used for UI customization and nothing is stored on our servers. You can use the "Clear History" nav option to remove all user settings and history.
